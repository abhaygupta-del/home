Second Mortgages

Second mortgages are a low-interest source of funds that you can use for debt consolidation and repayment of higher interest loans from other sources.

Second mortgages can also help you:

Buy a second property

Pay off high-interest loans and credit cards

Diversify your investment portfolio

Pay for school and education

If you have a mortgage on your home or another property and have paid down a part of that mortgage, you can take out a second mortgage on the property against the paid-down value as collateral. We have a simple three step program that has helped many Canadian homeowners just like you get out from underneath the high interest trap and rebuild your credit score.
